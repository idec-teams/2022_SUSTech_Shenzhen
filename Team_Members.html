OUR CREATIVE TEAM

Members

SUSTech_Shenzhen is a multidisciplinary team from China, but our values and energies are aligned.
We are all believers and excited about the results and impact of our Tyrian Purple Evolution (TPE) project.
Meanwhile, we have become a dual-time zone team since August, Pacific Time Zone (UTC-07:00) in California and China Standard Time (UTC+08:00) in Shenzhen.
Taking advantage of the time difference to take over can make us incredibly productive!
